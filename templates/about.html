Our Mission

The Cornell Maker Association (CMA) was originally conceived as a university-wide, cross-disciplinary initiative to foster innovation and creativity in the Cornell community. Founded by six undergraduates in the Engineering Leadership Program, CMA was created with the intention of providing Cornell students with the necessary resources to work on projects outside of their normal academic environment.
